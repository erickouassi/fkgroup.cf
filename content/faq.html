What is a Savings Group?

A Savings Group is a self-managed group of 2-25 individual members from within a family or community who meet regularly (weekly, biweekly, or monthly) to save their money in a safe space.

What is one benefit of participating in a Savings Group?

Savings Groups help members to save regularly in small, flexible amounts.

Do all members of the Savings Group have the same rights?

Yes, all members have the same rights in the Savings Groups.
